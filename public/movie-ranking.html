The Best Movies According to Renee

My top 3 movies of all time.

High School Musical

This movie has very good songs. It is also the beginning of one of the best trilogies in history.

Mulan

This was a very well animated movie. It had great songs too and it tells one of the most traditional stories of my people.

The Avengers: Endgame

Lots of my favorite characters died in this movie. It made me cry at the theater and moved me.
